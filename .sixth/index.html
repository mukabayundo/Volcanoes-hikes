Welcome to Rwanda Volcanoes Explorer

Rwanda is home to the magnificent Virunga Massif — a chain of ancient volcanoes straddling the borders of Rwanda, Uganda, and the DRC. This extraordinary region is one of Africa's most breathtaking natural wonders.

    Explore Volcano Activities

    🦍 Gorilla Trekking

    Trek through the misty slopes of the Virunga volcanoes to encounter endangered mountain gorillas.

    🌋 Mount Karisimbi Hike

    Summit the highest volcano in the Virunga chain at 4,507m through bamboo forests and alpine meadows.

    🐦 Nyiragongo View

    View the world's largest lava lake glowing from across the border — an unforgettable sight.

    🌿 Bisoke Crater Hike

    Hike to the stunning crater lake on Mount Bisoke through Volcanoes National Park.

    🔭 Astronomy Night

    Camp near Musanze and enjoy unobstructed stargazing under Africa's darkest skies near volcanic peaks.

    🏘 Iby'Iwacu Village

    Visit the cultural village near Volcanoes National Park to learn about Rwandan heritage.
